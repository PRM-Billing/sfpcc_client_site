Business goal (same as US-5 for Sleep Study): When a PFT or PST order is signed, CureMD should receive one PDF that contains the interpretation and the signed clinical report (interpretation pages first, then the report), not two separate uploads.

PFT / PST workflow (simplified):

  • Upload PFT/PST report → technician uploads source PDF.
  • Review & sign → physician completes interpretation and signs; system generates interpretation PDF and keeps signed report PDF.
  • Queue → queue_curemd_uploads row with two file paths.
  • CureMD bot → merges both PDFs and uploads one file to the patient chart folder.
Deployment baseline: origin/main at b1cc1ff (May 2026). Do not treat uncommitted local edits as shipped. Engineering implements, commits, and deploys R1–R4; QA proves in CureMD after deploy.
